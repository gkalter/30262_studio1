About Me

My name is Garren Kalter. I'm 22 years old and am originally from Chicago, IL. I am finishing up my senior year here at Clark and plan to complete the MS GIS fifth year program next year. While I have lots of academic interests, my formal academic background is a little more narrow. Come May, I'll have finished the undergraduate programs in Economics and Geography with concentrations in Ethics and Public Policy, Urban Geography, and a focus on Environmental Economics. My research focuses on transit optimization and I'm very excited to share the findings of my exploratory report with the city of Worcester once my thesis is complete. While at Clark, I've worn lots of hats. I've been a student representative, a beekeeper, an intramural athlete, a peer mentor, and an environmentalist. I really enjoy things like cooking, reading, exploring new parts of town, and having long intellectual conversations over coffee or tea.
